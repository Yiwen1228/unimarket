Policies

Privacy Policy

UofG UNIMarket collects only the personal information required to operate your account and process your orders: username, email address, and optional phone number. We do not share your data with third parties.

  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard one-way hashing and are never kept in plain text.
  • Session cookies are used solely for authentication and expire when you log out.
  • Chat messages are transmitted in real time and are not permanently stored on the server.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.

Terms of Use

  1. Eligibility — The platform is available to registered students and staff of the University of Glasgow.
  2. Account Responsibility — You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Do not share your account with others.
  3. Ordering — All orders are subject to product availability. Prices shown are in GBP and include VAT where applicable.
  4. Collection — Orders must be collected in person at the campus supermarket during opening hours.
  5. Acceptable Use — The live chat feature is provided for product enquiries and order support only. Abusive or inappropriate behaviour may result in account suspension.

Refund & Returns

  • Orders in Pending status can be cancelled by contacting staff via live chat.
  • Once an order is marked as Processing or Completed, cancellations are handled at the store counter.
  • Perishable goods (dairy, bakery) cannot be returned once collected.
  • Damaged or incorrect items will be replaced free of charge — please report issues within 24 hours.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making UNIMarket accessible to all users. Our platform follows WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines:

  • All interactive elements are keyboard-navigable with visible focus indicators.
  • ARIA labels and roles are used throughout for screen-reader compatibility.
  • Colour contrast ratios meet or exceed the 4.5 : 1 minimum for normal text.
  • Dynamic content regions use aria-live so assistive technologies announce updates.
  • The layout is fully responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes.
