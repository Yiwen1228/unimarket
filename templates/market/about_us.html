About Us

Who We Are

UofG UNIMarket is the official online marketplace for the University of Glasgow campus supermarket. We provide a centralised platform where registered students and staff can browse products, place orders, and communicate directly with our store team — all in one place.

Our Mission

We aim to replace disconnected tools such as spreadsheets, emails, and manual stock checks with a single integrated system. By automating inventory tracking and streamlining order processing, we help staff save time and reduce errors while giving customers a transparent, convenient shopping experience.

What We Offer

  • Product Browsing & Search — Find items quickly by name or category.
  • Online Ordering — Add items to your cart and place orders that are tracked from pending to completed.
  • Favorites — Save products you buy regularly for quick access.
  • Real-time Chat — Get instant support from staff via our built-in WebSocket chat.
  • Staff Dashboard — A unified control panel for order processing and inventory management.
